What is Fire Emblem?

Fire Emblem is a tactical RPG video game series developed by Intelligent Systems and published by Nintendo.

The goal of the player is to lead an army to battle, making strategic choices using an overhead grid-based map of the battlefield.

One of the gameplay's main trait is the permanent death of each character in battle, as each unit you control is a named character playing a part in the story. As of later entries, this feature can be turned off however.

As of now, there are 16 main games in the series, each having a different story and cast of characters - and you can learn more about those games here!
