Fire Emblem Heroes is a mobile game regrouping over 550 characters from the Fire Emblem franchise.

What is Fire Emblem Heroes?

Fire Emblem Heroes is a scaled-down, simplified version of the Fire Emblem games, developed for smartphones and played using simple and intuitive touch controls.

Designed to be easy to learn but hard to master, the game offers both a story-mode aventure as well as a great panel of additional game-modes earning you great rewards.
Some modes offer multiplayer interactivity, ranging from fighting alongside your friend's preferred units in Voting Gauntlets and Allegiance Battles to trying to defeat the defenses of other players in Aether Raids or Arena!

Fire Emblem Heroes classifies into what players call the "Gacha" category of games. The Heroes used for battle are obtained through "Summoning", events in which you obtain random characters from different rarities, ranging from 3 stars to 5 stars units.

However, summoning is not free, and requires "Orbs", an in-game currency. As the game is "free-to-start", you can obtain those orbs entirely freely over the course of gameplay and over time, but you can purchase additional orbs in the in-game shop if needed.
